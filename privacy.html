Privacy notice

This privacy notice explains how Follow-Up Fix handles business and quote-follow-up information for the launch service.

Who we are

Follow-Up Fix helps small home-service trades keep track of open quotes, missed enquiries, and review requests. Contact: ch3aly@googlemail.com.

What information we collect

  • Business contact details such as name, business name, email, phone, trade, town, and enquiry channel.
  • Client tracker details such as customer name, job type, quote value, quote date, follow-up date, channel, notes, and outcome.
  • Website enquiry details submitted through email links or onboarding forms.

Why we use it

  • To reply to enquiries and onboard clients.
  • To create and maintain a weekly follow-up tracker.
  • To prepare copy-paste follow-up messages for the client to send themselves.
  • To keep basic business records and improve the service.

What we do not do

  • We do not need access to your inbox, WhatsApp, phone, payment account, or social accounts.
  • We do not auto-send messages to your customers.
  • We do not sell customer data.

How long we keep data

We keep active client tracker data while the service is running. If a client cancels, they can request deletion of their tracker copy and working notes, subject to any records we need to keep for tax, accounting, dispute, or legal reasons.

Your rights

You can ask to access, correct, or delete personal information we hold about you. You can also object to direct marketing. You can complain to the UK Information Commissioner's Office if you are unhappy with how your information is handled.

Cookies

This launch site does not intentionally use analytics, advertising cookies, or tracking scripts. If analytics, ads, or embedded third-party tools are added later, this page and the cookie approach must be updated before launch.

This notice should be reviewed whenever new tools, processors, payment systems, analytics, or data retention practices are added.
