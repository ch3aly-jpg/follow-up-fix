Service terms

Launch terms for the £19/month Founding Plan.

The service

Follow-Up Fix provides a lightweight weekly follow-up system for small home-service trades. The service includes a tracker, message templates, and a weekly prompt list for open quotes, missed enquiries, and review requests.

Client responsibilities

  • The client sends all customer-facing messages from their own phone, email, or social account.
  • The client is responsible for the accuracy of quote values, customer details, and job notes they provide.
  • The client must only provide information they are allowed to share.

Price and cancellation

The Founding Plan is £19/month, cancellable anytime. Access, maintenance, and check-ins may stop when a payment fails or a subscription is cancelled.

No guarantee

Follow-Up Fix can help reduce forgotten follow-ups, but it does not guarantee replies, bookings, revenue, reviews, rankings, or customer outcomes.

Service boundaries

  • No auto-sending messages.
  • No inbox, WhatsApp, payment, or social account access.
  • No paid ads management.
  • No legal, tax, accounting, or compliance advice.

Changes

These terms may be updated as the service develops. Existing clients will be told about material changes before they take effect.
